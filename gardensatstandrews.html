The Gardens at St Andrew's Church

5 St Andrew Street, London, EC4A 3AF

The newly renovated Gardens of this Guild Church opened in September 2015. The award winning gardens are scattered with benches so you can easily find a quiet spot to enjoy your packed lunch. The church itself also remains open if you want to explore further. Our advice is to go for the Western facing side of the church - where it’s quieter and less hectic and you’re more likely to get a spot in the sunshine!
