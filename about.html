We are World Wide Women. A group of women from four different corners of the globe who want to put spontaneity back into your lunch hour.

Everyone has one but for whatever reason, time pressure, feeling like you should be working, or trying to impress your boss, people don’t take them. This website is to act as an inspiration for people and to nudge them to get out there and do whatever takes their fancy - as long as they’re doing it Out Of Office!
