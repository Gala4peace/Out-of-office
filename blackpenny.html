The Black Penny

34 Queen Street, London, WC2B 5AA

This is a fabulous coffee shop located in the heart of Covent Garden. You can either stop for a quick coffee or sit and enjoy their delicious healthly lunch options.

Black Penny is modelled on London's first coffee shop and 'penny universities', where a penny was the price of admission to the premises and gained you a cup of coffee as well as the opportunity to listen to and join in the debates of the day.
